## Overview

My personal portfolio and blog, built to share projects and writing.

## Tech stack

- **Framework**: Astro 5
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS v4
- **Content**: MDX + Astro Content Collections
- **Comments**: Giscus (GitHub Discussions)
- **Newsletter**: Buttondown
- **Hosting**: Netlify

## What I learned

Building a personal site is deceptively simple on the surface but gives you a lot of decisions to make.
I went with Astro because the content-first approach matched exactly what I needed —
static output, near-zero JavaScript by default, and excellent MDX support.
